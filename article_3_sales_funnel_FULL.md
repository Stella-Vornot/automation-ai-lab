## Step 1: Capture Leads (Website Form)

**What you need:**
- Landing page with form
- Form fields: Name, Email, Company, Phone, Interest

**Tools:**
- **Free:** Tally.so, Google Forms
- **Paid:** Typeform ($25/mo), JotForm ($24/mo)
- **Best value:** Tally (free tier sufficient)

**Setup time:** 30 minutes

**What happens next:** Form submission triggers Make.com webhook

---

## Step 2: Qualify Leads (AI Agent)

**What happens:**
New lead → AI calls them → Asks qualifying questions → Determines if good fit

**The AI asks:**
1. "What's your biggest challenge with [problem]?"
2. "How many [relevant metric] do you handle per month?"
3. "What's your timeline for solving this?"
4. "Are you the decision maker?"

**Tools needed:**
- **VAPI** ($0.05/min) — Best voice quality
- **Make.com** ($9/mo) — Orchestration
- **ChatGPT API** — Conversation logic

**Cost per call:** ~$0.15 (AI voice + GPT processing)

**Setup time:** 2-3 hours

---

## Step 3: Nurture Leads (Email Sequence)

**What happens:** Qualified leads enter 5-email sequence over 14 days

**Email 1 (Day 0):** Welcome + resource
*"Thanks for your interest! Here's the guide you requested..."*

**Email 2 (Day 2):** Case study
*"See how [Company] solved this exact problem..."*

**Email 3 (Day 5):** Educational
*"The #1 mistake people make with [problem]..."*

**Email 4 (Day 10):** Soft pitch
*"Ready to solve this? Here's how we can help..."*

**Email 5 (Day 14):** Direct CTA
*"Book a call to discuss your specific situation..."*

**Tools:**
- **Mailchimp** (Free up to 500 contacts)
- **ConvertKit** ($29/mo, better automation)
- **Beehiiv** (Free up to 2,500 subs)

**Setup time:** 3-4 hours (writing all 5 emails)

---

## Step 4: Book Meetings (Calendar Integration)

**What happens:** Interested leads click link → AI checks calendar → Books available slot → Sends confirmation

**The flow:**
1. Lead clicks "Book a Call" in email
2. Calendly/Cal.com shows available times
3. Lead selects time
4. Make.com triggers:
   - Add to Google Calendar
   - Send confirmation email
   - Add to CRM (Airtable/Notion)
   - Send reminder 24 hours before

**Tools:**
- **Calendly** (Free basic, $10/mo pro)
- **Cal.com** (Free, open-source)
- **Google Calendar** (Free)

**Setup time:** 1 hour

---

## Step 5: Follow-Up (Post-Meeting)

**What happens:** After meeting → Send proposal → Follow up → Close or nurture

**Automated sequence:**
- **Hour 0:** Send thank you + recap email
- **Day 1:** Send proposal (if qualified)
- **Day 3:** Follow up #1
- **Day 7:** Follow up #2
- **Day 14:** "Still interested?" email
- **Day 30:** Add to long-term nurture

**Tools:**
- **PandaDoc** (Proposals, $19/mo)
- **HubSpot** (CRM, Free tier)
- **Airtable** (CRM alternative, Free)

**Setup time:** 2 hours

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**❌ Mistake 1:** Building everything at once  
**✅ Fix:** Start with lead capture → AI qualification → Email. Add calendar later.

**❌ Mistake 2:** Writing generic emails  
**✅ Fix:** Personalize with lead's name, company, and specific pain point.

**❌ Mistake 3:** Not testing thoroughly  
**✅ Fix:** Run 20+ test calls before going live. Fix all bugs.

**❌ Mistake 4:** Ignoring data  
**✅ Fix:** Track conversion at every step. Optimize weakest link weekly.
