The Before State (January 2026)

I was stuck. As a solo consultant selling 1:1 services:

  • 8 manual sales calls per week (all me)
  • $8,000/month revenue — capped by my time
  • 60-hour work weeks
  • Hated prospecting, loved delivering

The bottleneck was me. To grow, I needed to either hire a sales rep ($4,000/month + training) or figure out automation. I chose automation.

My $10K Mistakes (Read This)

❌ Mistake #1: Automating Too Fast

I tried to automate everything in one week. System broke constantly, lost $3,000 in potential deals. Fix: Start with one piece, master it, then add the next.

❌ Mistake #2: Default AI Settings

Used VAPI's default voice. Sounded robotic, 60% hung up in 10 seconds. Fix: Clone your own voice, add pauses, test 50 calls before going live.

❌ Mistake #3: "Set and Forget"

I didn't check conversations for 3 weeks. AI was too aggressive, lost 40% of leads. Fix: Review 20 calls/week, optimize weekly. Not set and forget.

What I'd Do Differently

  1. Start smaller: Lead capture + email first. AI calls Week 2. Calendar Week 3.
  2. Track everything Day 1: I lost insights from first 200 leads.
  3. Hire help for integration: Spent 40 hours. Could have paid $500 to finish in 10.
  4. Review conversations weekly: Caught the aggressive tone in Week 2, not Week 4.
