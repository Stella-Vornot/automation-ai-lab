Your First Scenario

Goal: When someone fills out your form, send them a welcome email.

Step 1: Create Scenario

Log into Make.com → "Create new scenario" → Name it

Step 2: Add Trigger

Search your form tool → "Watch Responses" → Connect account

Step 3: Add Email Action

Click "+" → Search "Gmail" → "Send Email" → Compose
