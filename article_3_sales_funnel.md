# How to Automate Your Sales Funnel (Step-by-Step)

**Quick Guide:** Automate 80% of your sales process with AI + automation tools.

## The 5-Step Funnel:

1. **Capture** (Website form)
   - Use: Tally.so (free) or Typeform ($25/mo)
   - Time: 30 min setup

2. **Qualify** (AI voice agent)
   - Use: VAPI ($0.05/min) + ChatGPT
   - Cost: ~$0.15/call
   - Time: 2-3 hours setup

3. **Nurture** (Email sequence)
   - Use: Mailchimp (free) or Beehiiv (free)
   - 5 emails over 14 days
   - Time: 3-4 hours writing

4. **Book** (Calendar integration)
   - Use: Calendly (free) or Cal.com (free)
   - Time: 1 hour setup

5. **Follow-up** (Post-meeting)
   - Use: Airtable (free) or HubSpot (free)
   - Time: 2 hours setup

## Total Cost: $34-51/month
## ROI: Replace $4,000/month SDR
## Setup: 20 hours over 2 weeks

## Results Timeline:
- Month 1: $0 (setup)
- Month 2: $200-500
- Month 3: $500-1,000
