📥 Step 1: Capture Leads (Website Form)

📝

Goal: Convert visitors into leads

What you need: Landing page with a form
Form fields: Name, Email, Company, Phone, Interest/Pain Point
Tools:

  • Free: Tally.so, Google Forms
  • Paid: Typeform ($25/mo)
  • Our pick: Tally (free tier is plenty)

Setup time: 30 minutes
Key: Form submission triggers Make.com webhook instantly

🤖 Step 2: Qualify Leads (AI Voice Agent)

☎️

Goal: Determine if lead is worth your time

The flow: New lead submits form → AI calls within 2 minutes → Asks qualifying questions → Determines fit

AI asks:

  1. "What's your biggest challenge with [problem]?"
  2. "How many [relevant metric] do you handle per month?"
  3. "What's your timeline for solving this?"
  4. "Are you the decision maker?"

Tools needed:

  • VAPI — $0.05/min, best voice quality
  • Make.com — $9/mo, orchestration
  • ChatGPT API — ~$10/mo, conversation logic

Cost per call: ~$0.15 (voice + GPT)
Setup time: 2-3 hours
Result: Qualified leads move forward, unqualified get nurture sequence

📧 Step 3: Nurture Leads (Email Sequence)

📬

Goal: Stay top-of-mind until ready to buy

Qualified leads enter a 5-email sequence over 14 days:

  • Day 0: Welcome + valuable resource (no pitch)
  • Day 2: Case study (social proof)
  • Day 5: Educational content (establish expertise)
  • Day 10: Soft pitch ("Here's how we can help")
  • Day 14: Direct CTA ("Book a call to discuss")

Tools:

  • Mailchimp — Free up to 500 contacts
  • ConvertKit — $29/mo, better automation
  • Beehiiv — Free up to 2,500 subscribers

Setup time: 3-4 hours (writing all 5 emails)
Key stat: 40% of our bookings come from email, not AI calls

📅 Step 4: Book Meetings (Calendar Integration)

🗓️

Goal: Get qualified leads on your calendar

The flow:

  1. Interested lead clicks "Book a Call" in email
  2. Calendly shows available times
  3. Lead selects time
  4. Make.com triggers: Calendar event + Confirmation email + CRM entry + 24h reminder

Tools:

  • Calendly — Free basic, $10/mo pro
  • Cal.com — Free, open-source alternative

Setup time: 1 hour
Pro tip: Add pre-meeting questions to Calendly to qualify further
